FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
+
It depends on scope. A simple website takes 1–3 weeks. A full-stack web app typically takes 4–12 weeks. Enterprise platforms are planned in phases. We give you a detailed timeline during the discovery session before any work begins.
Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?
+
Yes! We work with clients globally. Communication happens via WhatsApp, Zoom, and email. Payments can be arranged via international wire transfer, Wise, or other agreed methods.
What technologies do you specialize in?
+
Our core stack includes React, Node.js, Python, Flutter, AWS, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and TensorFlow for AI. We select the best technology for each project's specific needs rather than forcing a one-size-fits-all approach.
Will I own the source code after delivery?
+
Absolutely. Full source code ownership is transferred to you upon project completion and final payment. We also provide documentation and a handover session so your team can maintain it independently.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?
+
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance retainers, bug fixing, feature additions, and server management. Enterprise clients receive a dedicated SLA with guaranteed response times. Contact us for a custom maintenance plan.
How do we start a project together?
+
Simply send us a WhatsApp message or fill out the contact form below. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs, then deliver a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing within 48 hours.
